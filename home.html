PROJECTS

Tourisum App

Upon opening the tourism app, users are warmly greeted by a user-friendly interface. They are provided with an input field to either enter their desired destination or choose from a curated list of popular places. Once a destination is selected, the app seamlessly retrieves comprehensive information, encompassing popular attractions, historical sites, local cuisine, and real-time weather updates. Users can further enhance their travel experience by planning personalized itineraries based on their preferences, duration of stay, and attraction operating hours. The app not only facilitates navigation through interactive maps but also ensures real-time guidance, preventing users from losing their way. To enrich the journey, a vibrant community aspect allows users to access and share recommendations, uncovering hidden gems, popular eateries, and unique experiences. Additionally, the app enables users to document their adventures through a built-in feature for photos and travel journaling. Prioritizing safety, the app keeps users informed about local guidelines and emergency contacts. For international travelers, a handy currency converter aids in effective budget management. Social connectivity thrives within the app's community forum, fostering a sense of camaraderie as users share valuable tips, experiences, and advice. The inclusion of a feedback and review system further contributes to a dynamic and helpful travel community.

Weather Website

Step into the realm of a captivating weather experience! Immerse yourself in this enchanting weather application where the simple act of entering a city name and triggering the "Get Weather" feature unlocks a world of real-time weather wonders from OpenWeatherMap, all orchestrated by the magical touch of JavaScript. This digital weather wizardry unfolds crucial details like the city name, temperature, weather icon, description, humidity, and wind speed. Fear not, for should a user stumble upon an invalid city or face an error, the app generously unveils a crystal-clear error message. The design, with its aesthetic charm showcased through a button and an input field, ensures a seamless and delightful entry into the captivating world of weather exploration
